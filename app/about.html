About my work and who I am

More about my work...

I am a self-taught front end developer with a passion for creating responsive, user-friendly websites and applications that work across all devices and browsers.


The main languages that I use are...

  • HTML5
  • CSS/Sass
  • JavaScript/jQuery

I love styling websites using Sass, togehter with the BEM naming convention and SMACSS architecture. I find this creates a well managed project and helps to deliver a well structured, easily maintainable website.
